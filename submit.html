Donate a Specimen

Found some in the wild? The museum is always accepting new specimens.

Photograph your find, tell us whose you think it is, and it may join the permanent collection — with your credit attached.

How a submission becomes an exhibit

  1. You submit. Attach a clear photo and your best species guess through a short form. Any common image type works.
  2. AI screens it. A vision model checks — is it actually feces? is the photo clear enough? does it plausibly match your guess? — and posts an advisory note. The AI only advises; it never has the final say.
  3. The curator reviews. A human approves or politely declines every submission.
  4. Approval publishes it. Approved photos are stripped of location metadata, credited to you, and dropped into the random rotation.
